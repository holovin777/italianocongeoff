### 3. Multiple Choice – Choose the Correct Pronunciation

   - **A)** AH-mor  
   - **B)** Ah-MOH-ray

2. Geoff tries to say *vino*. What’s the correct way to guide him?  
   - **A)** VEE-no  
   - **B)** VYE-no  
   - **C)** Veh-NEE-oh
