### Key Tips  

1. **C and G**  
   - Hard before *A, O, U*: *cane* (/ka-ne/), *gatto* (/ga-to/).  
   - Soft before *E, I*: *ciao* (/t Éao/), *giorno* (/d íorno/).  

2. **S and Z**  
   - *S*: Voiceless /s/ (e.g., *sole*) or voiced /z/ (e.g., *casa*).  
   - *Z*: Voiceless /ts/ (e.g., *zucchero*) or voiced /dz/ (e.g., *mezzo*).
