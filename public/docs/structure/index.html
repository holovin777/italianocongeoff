      Italian Lessons – Italiano con Geoff #

      Welcome to Italiano con Geoff! Here’s a full learning journey that covers everything from beginner basics to advanced conversational skills. Each lesson builds on the previous, guiding you toward fluency in a fun and engaging way.

      Beginner Level #

      1. Basic Greetings – Simple greetings like “Ciao” and “Buongiorno.”
      2. Introducing Yourself – How to introduce yourself and ask for someone’s name.
      3. Ordering at a Café – Essential phrases for ordering food and drinks.
      4. Asking for Directions – Learn to ask and understand directions.
      5. Shopping Essentials – How to ask prices, sizes, and common phrases for shopping.
      6. Numbers and Counting – From 1 to 100, plus tips on dates and times.
      7. Days of the Week & Time – Talking about days, months, and telling time.
      8. Basic Pronunciation – Key tips for pronouncing Italian vowels and consonants.
      9. Talking About Family – Introducing family members and talking about relationships.
      10. At the Restaurant – Ordering food, handling the bill, and Italian dining etiquette.
      11. Making Small Talk – Phrases for casual conversation and breaking the ice.
      12. Describing Weather – Common phrases for discussing weather and seasons.

      Intermediate Level #

      1. Describing People and Places – Using adjectives to describe appearance and locations.
      2. Expressing Preferences – Talking about likes, dislikes, and preferences.
      3. Basic Grammar: Present Tense Verbs – Introduction to regular and irregular verbs in the present tense.
      4. Talking About Hobbies – Discussing interests and activities you enjoy.
      5. Transportation & Travel – Phrases for taking a taxi, train, or bus.
      6. Health and Emergencies – Essential phrases for doctor visits or emergencies.
      7. Shopping for Clothes – Asking about sizes, colors, and trying on clothes.
      8. Talking About Daily Routine – Phrases for describing your daily activities.
      9. Italian Holidays & Traditions – Overview of Italian holidays and cultural phrases.
      10. Basic Grammar: Past Tense – Introduction to the past tense for storytelling.
      11. Social Media & Technology Vocabulary – Common phrases for modern conversations.
      12. Making Plans with Friends – Inviting, accepting, and suggesting activities.

      Advanced Level #

      1. Discussing Opinions – How to express your opinions politely.
      2. Formal vs. Informal Language – Using the right formality in different situations.
      3. Talking About Future Plans – Discussing upcoming events and future aspirations.
      4. Grammar: Future Tense – Introduction to future tense verbs.
      5. Expressing Emotions in Depth – Advanced phrases for conveying emotions.
      6. Idiomatic Expressions – Common Italian idioms and their meanings.
      7. Job and Career Vocabulary – Useful phrases for work and professional settings.
      8. News and Current Events – Vocabulary and phrases for discussing the news.
      9. Grammar: Conditional Tense – Using conditional tense for hypothetical situations.
      10. Debating & Agreeing/Disagreeing – Phrases for friendly debates and discussions.
      11. Italian Humor & Sarcasm – Understanding humor, jokes, and casual banter.

      Fluency Level #

      1. Storytelling in Italian – Practice narrative techniques and sharing personal stories.
      2. Italian Literature & Film – Key phrases and vocabulary for discussing Italian culture.
      3. Formal Writing & Email Etiquette – Writing formally for work or personal correspondence.
      4. Advanced Grammar: Subjunctive Mood – A deep dive into the subjunctive.
      5. Conversational Practice – Exercises and practice for natural, flowing conversations.

      Each lesson will help build your Italian language skills step-by-step. Whether you’re ordering coffee or debating current events, these lessons are designed to make you feel comfortable and confident in Italian.
